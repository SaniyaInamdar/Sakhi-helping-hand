Who We Are

We are Sakhee - Helping Hand, a platform created to support working women by simplifying their daily lives. Our goal is to connect them with trusted service providers for tasks like household chores, childcare, and meal preparation.

At Sakhee, we also empower unemployed individuals by offering them opportunities to provide these services. It’s a community built on trust and convenience, making life easier for women while creating jobs for others.

  • Home Services: Maid, cleaning, and cooking assistance.
  • Child Services: Trusted childcare and babysitting.
  • Prepared Meals: Fresh, healthy food delivered.
  • Learning Services: Driving lessons and skill-based support.
  • Emergency Services: Quick help in urgent situations..
  • Verified Workers: Reliable and trustworthy professionals.
